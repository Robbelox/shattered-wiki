Alchemy
Alchemy

The art of using materials, mundane or magical, in order to concoct brews for a variety of effects. This is most frequently seen through potions, and can be applied to a target through digestion, injection, or contact.

Enchantment
Enchantment

Relying on ancient runes (base galactic) of unknown origin, Enchantment can infuse objects, tools, and weapons either through directly carving specific rune combinations onto the surface of the object, or by transferring the power of a rune combo onto it. Runes must be extremely specific, and even the smallest error in their writing can render the enchantment dysfunctional. Books can be an excellent form of storage for specific combinations of runes to later be grafted to their final target.

Inherent
Inherent

The most ambigeous form of magic, little is known about it. It is thought magic that requires no specific ingredients or source falls into this category, such as portals, the Shatterings, and the Culling.

Sorcery
Sorcery

A form of magic lost to time, with little to no modern practitioners. It said they were capable of casting 'spells' without the aid of enchantment or alchemy, though the validity of these tales are extremely unreliable. It is said those who practiced were the first to travel the dimensions, and constructed not only the ancient strongholds and their dormant portals to the End, but the original gateway connecting Ayka and Verdan. They supposedly died out centuries before the first Shattering.

Void Magic
Void Magic

A little known form of magic, undiscovered as its own classification by humanity. It is not native to Ayka and Verdan, and only found among the mindless Ender. It is extremely dangerous.

Redstone
Redstone

About

A naturally occurring material that is inherently magically charged. Unlike electricity, it's charge can be maintained by passively absorbing faint magical energies in the environment. It can be toxic if ingested in large quantities, but in smaller amounts has been used as a strong spice on foods and in drinks.

Electricity

Despite misconceptions, electricity and redstone are almost entirely unrelated. Redstone is capable of holding a brief electrical charge, however it is extremely inefficient at conducting electricity.

Sickness

It is not uncommon for those who often work with redstone in enclosed spaces to inhale it. If not properly prevented or treated, these redstone particles can clump and re-crystalize, creating long-lasting respiratory issues. In severe cases, this can lead to death.

Alchemy Uses

When used in brewing potions, redstone powder is known to extend the duration of the concoction. This is done by acting as a form of magical sponge, absorbing the effect of the brew, and releasing that effect over a longer period of time as the body metabolizes the dust.
