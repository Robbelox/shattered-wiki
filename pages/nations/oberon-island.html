Oberon Island

About

Oberon's Island is an island in Verdan, raised from the earth by Oberon himself as neutral ground for all nations. Whatever the political climate of the Kingdoms, all are welcome here.

As the world around him grew, so did the island. The Emerald Bank became a centre of economy for Verdan, the Diplomatic Centre a space for political meetings, and the Markets a hub for worldwide trade. Under Oberon's watch, the island has continued to flourish as a place of peace.

At its centre lies the last standing portal to the nether. When the others were found to be growing unstable—drawing dangerous elements from other worlds into Verdan—it was decreed that this alone would remain. The scattered remnants of the rest have since been cleared away, leaving little trace of what once stood.
