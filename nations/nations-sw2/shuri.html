The Empire of ShÅ«ri

The people of Shuri were born from the other realm, the warrior people of a foreign world. After being stranded in this new and bountiful land, they immediately sought to take it for their own. They banded together and claimed a large island quite close to the divine portal that they had come through.

They started to refine the land, creating food for their people and weapons for their army. It looked like it wouldn't be long until they conquered the entirety of this new world, but with every great empire comes great tragedy.

A group of power-hungry warriors wanted more. They staged a revolution, killing multiple mighty generals under the cover of night. They hung these heads outside their windows, showing mighty gestures of war. And so they fought, tearing the once mighty empire into shreds.

After centuries passed of constant battle, the people were starting to grow tired of war. It was a waste of resources and destruction of entire cities. The warriors didn't want to fight anymore, and so a new leader appeared to usher them into an new era - an era of peace.
