The Lands of Zurtu

The natives of this land had few settlements, but none were as small as the northern tribes. Their land was dry and barren, and every year was a struggle to survive.

That all changed when the portal was opened. A few farmers and labourers travelled to the desert in search of prosperity and away from the domineering Shuri. They found this poor tribe and took them under their wings, teaching them techniques and helping them prosper.

The people started to hear rumours of more war in the south, they then hid themselves away further into the treacherous desert. Many people searched for them, but they had long since disappeared.

Recently however, the tribe has decided to reveal itself to the world, with both the Shuri and the Valdelor being heavily crippled, they deemed it safe to appear.
