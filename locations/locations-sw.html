Seurri is the capital city of the Prythonian Kingdom. Currently it is one of the most populous cities, having houses for almost all Prythonian citizens (some choose to live in other cities in the kingdom).

The first citizens of the city were: King Cedric, Uchdryd, Maeveen, Rihannon, Devona and Gildas.

Initially the center of the city was the area now know as Old village area, but due to having only starter tools, King Cedric decided to move the city center near the coast.

Seurri is split into 8 districts: City Center, Arena Area, Old Village, Bothain Area, Robtern's Area, Mystic Forest, Druid Area & Northern Gate

Tiberia
Faction: Tiberia
X: -2060
Z: -1785

About

The Tiberian Empire was the biggest empire in the Ayka Continent. It was the first and most affected by The Shattering.

Currently its progressor is the post-shattering nation, Varian Empire.

History

It started as a small Achaean colony founded in 757 BS by the Achaean Commander Lepidus, later changing his name to Commander Manius.

In 643 BS the colony managed to conquer 2 neighboring colonies then it started to expand. In 623 BS it declared its independence under the name of Tiberian Kingdom. In 621 BS it changed its name in Tiberian Empire.

Demography

The Tiberian Empire had one of the most diverse populations because of conquering different tribes and nations.

However, some of the biggest minorities were Achaeans and Prythonians.
