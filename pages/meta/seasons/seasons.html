Season 2

World of Verdan

Before Season 2, about four hundred years after the Great Shattering, a portal to the uninhabited Realm of Verdan was discovered. After six hundred years of settlement, the players establish three new nations on the Three Continents of Verdan. [More...]

Season 3

Night of the Culling

In Season 3, the players deal with the aftermath of the Culling. This catastrophic Shattering, which occurred nine hundred years after the Great Shattering, claimed many lives at the hands of spectral reapers. [More...]

Season 4

Shattered Crown of Quart

Before Season 4, the Crown of Quart made itself vulnerable to Shatterings by digging past bedrock, breaking the ancient protective barrier. After witnessing the Culling and two Shatterings, the nation has weakened enough for new players to lay claim to the continent. [More...]
