Aisha Licho

About

Aisha was a redheaded central fae with striking green eyes. Her hair changed shades of red due to climate and seasons. She appeared to be in her 20s, but she had actually lived for over 2 thousand years, barely tapping into her lifespan's potential.

She was a kind, outgoing, yet reserved woman. She was kind to strangers, quick to care and difficult to anger. However, she was slow to forgive once wronged. She tended to give people the benefit of the doubt.

Biography

Origins

Aisha Licho was born in a small central fae town, outside the protection of the kingdom of Maria, to the Lichos — a respected family in her town. She was sickly for the first couple hundred years of her life, but eventually befriended Kato Dira, her best friend and closest confidant for many hundreds of years. Due to the fae not having proper sex ed — at least not in their hometown — the two ended up accidently having a child, Avie Licho. Sadly, Aisha's entire world came crashing down around her when her entire village was murdered in a raid for their wings, leaving Aisha the only known survivor. She wandered Iteria for hundreds of years, lost in her grief. She eventually found herself around Nindal, where she was found by the Sakari Order's leader, Wilhelm. She joined the Sakari Order, ready for a sense of purpose and belonging, which is where her story started.

Sakari Order Life

After joining the Sakari Order, Aisha's life was fairly uneventful. She met new people, farmed in her little farm, and created a home for herself. She even became pretty good at brewing. She began to come to full terms with the loss she'd been hiding inside her heart, as her wings grew back and she became at peace again. This peace lasted for a while, her new home becoming perfect and routine. Much of the Sakari Order "went on an expedition" (read- stopped playing on Iteria for a while), and Aisha stayed, greeting anyone who came to Avisburg with a smile.

That is, until the appearance of one Andrew of the Brotherhood. Andrew came into her life claiming he would take over Avisburg, and threatened her life (I think it was canonically in front of Teri). Somewhere during this time, Aisha saw her friend Ghostbelio die permanently, killed by Niko in front of her and Teri.
