# Ondor ## About === --- ## History ### Culling (900) Main Article: [[Culling]] - - - ### Quarti Civil War (900 - 1120) Main Article: [[Quarti Civil War]] The [[Quarti Empire]] was consumed in war, a time of conflict and warring peoples that lasted for hundreds of years. As provinces turned state, the once-great Empire fracturing like glass, there were those who wanted no part in it. One such group was House Ondor, one of the high houses of the Empire, who wished to spare the common man from suffering in a war driven by greed. The head of House Ondor left an open invitation, welcoming any and all regardless of sex, gender, caste, or wealth, to join them in the [[Anquandes]]. Using the mountain range as a natural barrier, the people under House Ondor’s protection managed to live relatively peaceful lives. As the years passed, the unnamed colony was given the name of “Ondor” by the people, wishing to honour their saviours. As more people heard of a sanctuary among the mountains, Ondor expanded, turning from colony to two to a dozen, all under the collective name of Ondor. Every colony worked with one another, creating an intricate web of aid and trade throughout the mountains and valleys. The land itself, the mountains and the valleys, gave the people their tranquillity. In thanks, the Ondorans held great reverence for the land itself, the very dirt upon which they tread, in stark contrast to their Quarti ancestors. For over a hundred years the Ondorans thrived in their secluded slice of the [[quart|Sundered Lands]], yet the most recent [[shattering]] twenty years ago, spelled disaster for Ondor. In the quakes, the members of House Ondor were relieved of their lives, leaving the colonies and clans disorganised and unruly. Without the order and organization of their leadership, trade routes swiftly broke down, and famine broke out. Many tried and failed to unite the withering nation.
