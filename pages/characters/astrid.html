Biography

Origins

Astrid had no memory of her family, and her memories of her childhood were rather hazy. Astrid was found and raised in a convent from 10-17 years old. The convent was strict and very quiet, ranging from being locked in a small room to being beaten regularly by a bitter priest in order to ‘straighten her up.’ While Astrid looked after the convent, the priest would allow her to work in the garden if he was in a good mood. She became obsessed with designing layouts, as the priest had noticed himself. He allowed her to try out architecture, and was surprised at her new-found skill. Astrid was sent off to work with a fully experienced builder, and stayed there until the Great Shattering happened. She escaped to Ormsa, following Haradlr and his group.

Astrid’s hair turned stark white on her 18th birthday for an unknown reason, and eventually faded to a light blonde.
