Biography

Origins

Acionna was raised by a Prythonian archer named Briana, who unfortunately died after the Shattering when Acionna was still fairly young and immature. She had once wanted to become a druid, but decided she would not try to become one after her mother died. Acionna decided she would find her own unique place in the new world and society they lived in, and wanted to be as independent as possible. She was ready to help the kingdom and its people in any way that would benefit. She became a diplomat.
