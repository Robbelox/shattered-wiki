Biography

Origins

Artemios grew up in a small, secluded island north of Achaea. She lived in a small village with her family. It was just Artemios, her father, mother and some farm animals. Her favorite memory back on the island was when she had gone fishing with her father at the age of 7. She remembers how beautiful it looked, as they had left just as the sun went down. Her father was trying to teach her to fish, but Artemios had other ideas like watching the fish, and begging her dad to let them go. As they ventured out into the sea, looking for an area with a lot of fish, Artemios stared down at the colorful ocean with various fishes and turtles passing by. The young girl giggled at the sight of a new creature. It was one she hadn't seen before, it was a grey animal that had a cute noise. As it got closer, she gasped as she got the Dolphin's Grace effect. It made a cute noise and pushed the boat faster. "Daddy! Look!"

After that day, Artemios became fond of dolphins. She admired them deeply. She grew up happily, until the age of 15. Her parents were growing older and they didn't want Artemios to live her life stuck on a boring island. They sent her away to hopefully give her a great life, but to also have the possiblity of some new wealth. They didn't have the money to come along on the journey.

They gathered supplies for months for her to travel somewhere. While she boated all the way to the place she would find out to be Achaea, she had turned 16.

Artemios lived a happy life in Achaea with her husband and tended to her ranch. She did not have a job, but considered herself a happy baker and archer. After the loss of her dogs Coco and Puppy, she became very caring towards her animals and respected them deeply.
