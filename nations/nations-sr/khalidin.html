While Khalidin had learned to endure the Shatterings, the Culling brought a new threat. Entire temples and palaces were obliterated, and much of the precious materials and crafts the people valued so much were lost in the mayhem.

What few people remained took it upon themselves to elect a leader, a style of leadership which had never ruled before. Typically the form of leadership in Khalidian was a sultan who passed on their title to their first born. This time an elected leader was needed to unite what few of their people remained. For the first time in centuries, Khalidin had to pick up their roots and move to find a perfect place on which they would be able to regrow safely.
