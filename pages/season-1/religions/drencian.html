Drencian

The Galatai people worship the gods of nature, mainly those of the forests, rivers and highlands that they inhabit.

Those who have a close connection with the gods are chosen to become Druids, who use natural goods to create items to bring the human mind closer to the realm of the gods. These items are usually inhaled or ingested, and induce a magical state that distorts your view of the world, allowing some to even speak with divine beings.

Due to this connection with the gods, the Druids hold a high place in the Galatai society, and are often at the left hand of the king to provide divine judgement.
