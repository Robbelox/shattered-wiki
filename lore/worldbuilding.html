Realms
Realms

About

The collective cluster of dimensions forming a single world.

Binary Realms

About

A pair of two linked Realms, akin to binary stars. Ayka and Verdan are examples of such Realms. Mythology states that Ayka and Verdan were twin sisters, goddesses of life. Verdan was peaceful and mischievous, creating life and playing pranks on her sister. Ayka was more immature, prone to violence, and easily enraged by her sister's antics. However, this mythology is virtually non-existent in present time, with other gods of varying reality belonging to other religions appearing throughout history. They may have methods to travel between them.

Ayka

The Realm and setting of Shattered World, Realms and Crown, which for the past thousand years has had centennial Shatterings (with year 1000 being replaced by the Culling).

Verdan

The Realm and setting of SW2. Before settlers ventured from Ayka, Verdan had no known sapient inhabitants, and is not cursed with Shatterings.

Iteria

A realm far away from the previously-mentioned binary realms. Only a couple of people have ever managed to travel to this one by mistake. Some characters seem to have existed in both.

True Void
True Void

The empty nothingness between Realms, nearly impossible to travel to or through. Home of the Conqueror.

Ancient Cities
Ancient Cities

About

Compounds created by those who wished to travel between Realms using a powerful portal. Due to portals traversing the True Void being impossible to create or sustain without the Conqueror's influence, the portals backfired and spit void back out, melding with the people and other arcane experiments in the cities - creating the Sculk.

Sculk

A fungal/moss-like growth, created by Void Magic sustaining off the souls of those in the cities, which has since grown into the biome known as the Deep Dark.

The Conqueror
The Conqueror

A conflict goddess, and the only known being known to be capable of surviving and thriving in the True Void unaided. Goal is to tear apart Realms from the inside out, using the remains to build a pseudo-Realm of her own to call home.

The Ender Dragon(s) and Endermen are under her control/influence. If one is to somehow infuse themselves with Ender, they may be also be prone to her influence.

Big Events

War for the Nether
War for the Nether

The War

Roughly two hundred years prior to the first Shattering, the Ender invaded the Nether, attempting to terraform the dimension and wipe out the scattered clans of Piglins. For many years, the Piglins were decimated, before eventually uniting under a singular clan. This clan's leader won several key battles, and in time the united front of the Piglins drove out the invaders.

The Aftermath

The hero of the war was made king of the newly united Piglins across the Nether. However, he was only suited to wartime, and his kingdom fell to revolt and civil war not two decades after being founded. After being imprisoned by the enraged population, he was set to a public execution. However, he went missing from his cell not long before it was scheduled, and was not heard from again. The Bastions created for the war fell into disrepair, now only inhabited by those who dare stake a claim over the buildings. Many Piglins dare not try, considering the old war bases cursed. Warped Forests are a result of Ender terraforming, a hybridization of chorus and crimson.

Shatterings
Shatterings

The Shattering was an event of quakes and plagues, which spawned the first undead and monsters of Ayka's Overworld. They happen once every century, essentially kneecapping civilization and preventing them from creating permanent advancements beyond their current technological prowess.

The Culling
The Culling

On the thousandth year, the Shattering was replaced with the Culling, a night in which spectral reapers ravaged the peoples of Ayka with no mercy.
