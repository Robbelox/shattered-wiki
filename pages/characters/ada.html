Biography

Origins

Abandoned by his parents, Ada was raised near the ocean alongside many turtles. Up to age 9, he lived by the turtles' ways and munched on kelp and seaweed. Eventually, he was taken in by Bastaernia, and found himself a lovely Achaean wife. His most important goal became to be the most experienced man in the world.
