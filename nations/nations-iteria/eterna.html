Eterna

The Empire of Eterna was created not too long ago, when Wilhelm of the Sakari order and Julius, Architect in the Sect of the Fallen One at that time, talked about whether a new faction should be created. Julius was unsure at first, but could not seem to get rid of the idea. So when the time was right, he gathered people and founded Eterna.

Not soon after, most members of the Sect followed him, the Sect was no longer an independent faction.

The main goal of Eterna is growth. Attracting people, and building towns and cities across Iteria with the idea to allow people to do pretty much whatever they like and also to encourage trading. However, it was clear from the beginning that a military force - if not at least for defensive purposes - would be required. These goals were fairly ambitious, and it was also clear that it would take some time to get there.
