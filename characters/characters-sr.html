Callie
Callie Seline
883 -

Species: Human
Gender: Female

Kingdom: Hammaris

Played By: Letoius

Callie Seline was born to a nomadic Hammarish clan roughly a decade and a half prior to the Culling. In her younger life, she was often treated with disrespect by her clanmates. However, when she was around 9 years old, Callie's innate talent for fishing with both rod and spear were discovered, and combined with her lower weight, allowed her to fish on ice too thin for the others. For the next few years, she would become one of the better providers of food for her part of the clan, and earned the respect, whether it be begrudgin or earnest, of her fellow Hammarish.

Several years before the Culling, when Callie was 13 years old, a band of seafares flying an unkown flag ambushed the tribe in the night. Most warriors were slain, but the women, children, and those skilled in other manners were taken captive, forced to work at their camp. Being too far inland for ocean fishing, Callie was put to work mining surface-level rocks. Callie grew increasingly jaded, and her dependecy on alcohol from her youth grew even stronger, drinking whatever she could get her hands on. When Callie was 16, and the Culling swept across the world, those who held her captive were all but wiped out, her own survival nothing more than luck. In the chaos, she ran.

In months that followed, Callie learned to get what she needed to survive, often through less than respectable means. Despite this, her coin purse never gained much heft, as whatever money she was able to aquire was swiftly loss to a local tavern. It was not until several months ago that Callie began to recover, when Accalia found her passed out drunk not far off a path, and took the girl under her wing.

Riad
Riad Mamun
~855 -

Species: Human
Gender: Male

Kingdom: Khalidin

Played By: Oi_Boy

Before the Culling Riad was a well known business man with his hand often on gold filled scales. He had previously been a high treasurer to the ruling Sultan and watched over much of the old nations gold supplies.

Riad is like many of the native Khalidians, his skin is warm and his hair is dark. Even in his ageing years his hair has stayed the rich colour he was born with and has yet to begin to salt and pepper. Matching the dark hair on his head, is the dark hair that paints his face; Cut neatly beneath his nose and framing his cheeks is a thick beard which curls up cheekily as he smirks. In the year since the Culling he has been able to reproduce and repair some of his older garments and is frequently seen in differing shades of blue, a colour he says brings out the green in his eyes and the gold of his earrings. His earrings are his most treasured possession as they were made from the first pieces of gold he ever came upon when he was a boy.

Riad first and foremost is charismatic and secretive. Gold is money, info-textrmation is wealth. Any chance for some nugget of knowledge is one Riad will happily take. With this in mind, Riad wouldn't let anyone pass up the opportunity to pay with info-textrmation. While he is charismatic and can talk the hind legs off a donkey, he is never without mystery. He can disappear for days at a time and return as if he had never left, simply saying he "was out on business". In combative situations Riad will always try using his words to settle the disagreement, wanting to avoid physical fighting until absolutely necessary. He may even invoke the assistance of others to hide his tracks as he leaves, or use curious potions to allow him to disappear completely.

Riad plays both sides of the coin, weighing up the perfect balance of both the current situation and those to come. This can be a boon or a curse as he tries to work out moves ten steps in advance. His strength lies in what he knows but doesn't tell, leaving much to the imagination of others. Riad's weaknesses can be in his physical ability and often distaste for himself to be violent, because of this he tries to find ways out of those situations and could possibly be read as weak.

His main skills are those in deception and observation. He also does well with planning and finding gold.
