Biography

Backstory

In a time since forgotten, not long after the fall of the Vi Empire, a young girl known as Vivian Kal had her village raided by bandits. The men were slaughtered, while the women and children were taken captive. However, Vivian's time in captivity did not last long, for she was swiftly bought by a man she had hoped to be her saviour; a powerful arcanist, experimenting in the practice of necromancy in the hopes of achieving immortality. His hopes were that, by finding a way to track exactly where souls went after death, he would be able to recall a soul back to its mortal flesh from it's resting place in the beyond. In time, he determined he would need to infuse the soul with a material that could transcend dimension and plane, in order to summon it forth.

Vivian was the first soul subject to his experiments after making this revelation. It took many long months, but in time he successfully infused the girl's spirit utilizing the crushed extract from ender pearls. The only thing left was for her to die, so he could recall her. However, the experiments proved a failure, and after being murdered, Vivian's soul was set adrift, drawn not it's natural resting place, but to the dimension who's energies had been infused within her: the End.

Unable to be recalled, unable to pass on, Vivian wandered the desolate wastelands of the End for longer than any mortal should, her mind fracturing and breaking and dumping everything it needed to in order to survive. For countless years, the broken, spectral husk of a woman sat alone - until someone, or rather something, was able to put her back together. Her past forgotten, Vivian, or Vivi, was visited by the voice of the deity known as the Conqueror, an extra-dimensional goddess of the Void. Needing a follower, an acolyte to enact her will, the faint whispers of the Conquer led the girl to a peculiar, black and purple crystal. Now in possession of a Void Crystal, a powerful and rare phenomenon caused by pooling void magics, the Conqueror's voice became clear. The deity, to whom Vivi owed her repaired mind, guided the spectre, the Voidwalker, to freedom - a return to the Overworld.

The woman, rebuilt as the Conqueror needed, was cruel and tactical, and of a mind dedicated solely to the summoning of her Goddess. When entering a society so unfamiliar to her, Vivi used her diminutive voice to her advantage, playing dumb in order to earn the charity of others, all the while constructing labs and running her own experiments in order to, one day, summon her goddess. While she created many alliances and had many deals, her only allegiance has ever belonged to herself and her goddess.
