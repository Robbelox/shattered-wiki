Biography

Origins

Æthelstan was born to a small farming family in the Bastaerni lands. Throughout the ages, his family had attended to this small farming estate, tending to crops, trading those crops to whoever controlled the land at the time (which changed frequently), and becoming victims to several raids from rival tribes (which happened even more frequently). Despite this, he seemed to have a tongue of silver and could talk his way out of most situations. Many times he was a mediator between the tribes, settling uneasy peace.

For a while, Æthelstan had aspirations like most low-born boys his age: to become a knight, fight great battles, and earn glory and gold. His uncle Alfrid had done the same when he was but a baby, and when he visited his brother Æthelstead, he told the best stories. Later, Æthelstan grew out of his aspirations of becoming a knight when his father's health began to wane; he'd take his father's position for him.

The Great Shattering hit hard. His father, whose health was already failing, became a victim of one of the great plagues. His mother was bedridden for weeks on end, and no matter how hard Æthelstan tried to care for her, she only got worse. Then young Æthelstan contracted it too, and he thought that he too would perish in the midst of this plague. He pushed through and came out on the other side with interesting news. In all the chaos, some local Bastaerni tribes united to form the kingdom of Osterwalde. Æthelstan pledged his allegiance to the reigning monarch and began the task of rebuilding his once great estate, realizing that most other farms had been destroyed.
