Biography

Origins

Arne grew up in a rural area with his mother, father and 3 brothers. His dad was a hunter and fighter, which led to his eventual death. When Arne was 11, his dad taught him basic things like how to shoot a bow, use a sword, ride a horse, etc.

Arne was not much of an architect but was good with his hands. He worked on a small farm for a number of years and was then hired in a mine full-time so he could pay the rent for his family. Their only food was wheat, potatoes, carrots, and beef from the few cows they owned.

One day, in the middle of the night, an unknown group of armoured men came to their house, posing as the land owner, and they wanted rent. Arne explained that he had just payed the rent and couldn't afford to pay it again. The men then killed their cows and burned their crops and house down, killing his youngest brother. When the other villagers came to help the family, the men fled.

In the morning, Arne started to look for a new settlement. They tried to travel far away north to hide from the unknown men. After many weeks of searching, his 2 remaining brothers died of hunger and flu. Eventually Arne reached snow.

They had infinite water by melting the snow, and were happy. When they went to sleep however, the fire burned faster than expected. Arne's mother froze to death. Arne was scared and heartbroken, so he ran as far and as fast as he could.

The next night while looking for a settlement, a pack of wolves spotted Arne so he ran and hid in a cave. He woke up fine the next morning to a group of men, armoured, but not like the "unknown men". They helped him get up and brought him out of the cave. He turned around to see a settlement known as Kurgan.
