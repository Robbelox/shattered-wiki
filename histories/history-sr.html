The Story of our Shattered Realm

Roughly a millennium ago, the world was struck by a horrific tragedy; the event shook the land itself and formed vast cracks in the earth. Brought forth were plagues that swept the lands, and monsters that clawed and dragged their way to the surface.

The event became known as the Shattering.

From the mightiest empires to the smallest clans, all were affected. The people were slaughtered en masse by the horrors that blanketed the world.

As the decades passed, the world began to rebuild. Nations that had persisted for thousands of years prior, since time immemorial, remained strong and true.

As many had privately feared, the denizens of the world could not hold the peace. A hundred years after the Shattering, and each and every century that followed, the Shattering would rear its head once again to inflict pain and destruction.

As time passed the people grew less and less afraid. Not only could they rebuild with ease, but also prepare for the recurring event. Adjusting to the undead and other horrors stalking the lands, the average person grew more fit and able. Many had learned to wield a blade in combat.

In the wilds of the world, away from the now thriving civilizations, a band of explorers stumbled across a unique structure. Upon return, they boasted of their discovery to all who would listen.
