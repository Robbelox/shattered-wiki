Thinking Leuteuh is preparing for an offensive strike, the Vi Empire attacks Leuteuh, not taking time to conquer, but instead complete annihilation. Lizardkin population is driven dangerously low
Vi Empire officially becomes the strongest faction in all of Iteria, with the most land and the ability to keep it protected.
Vi Empire moves for world domination via eradication of lesser kingdoms. Severe nationalism is prevalent among violent Vi, though many still take after the more peaceful ways of the Eliseu Vi
On their conquest, the Vi Empire uses non-Vi as their warriors, both taking down potential opposition, and bringing competing species to more manageable numbers
Peaceful Vi, seeing the destruction of other species, begin rioting for the protection and preservation of these species and their cultures
Huge divide is formed within Vi empire, as a civil rights movement morphs into civil war
Carnage and destruction is so widespread, the massive Vi empire is brought down in a mere decade.
The innate differences in peaceful Vi and violent Vi finally grow enough for the two ways of life to be considered separate species; Villagers and Illagers
Villagers conceal their magic, and learn to fear violence in the ruins of the Vi Empire. Settle down in small towns that dot the landscape.
Illagers, who have all but lost their magic except for a small few, begin constructing new bases of operations and outposts, their nationalism now evolved into complete xenophobia, wish to re-conquer Iteria and completely eradicate any other species.
