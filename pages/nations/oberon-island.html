Oberon Island

History

Oberon's Island was grown straight from the earth to be a space for all nations to work together. No matter the social or political climate of the Kingdoms, all were welcome to Oberon's home and be on neutral ground.

As the world around Oberon grew, so did his island; things were built so that Kingdoms could come together in peace. The Emerald Bank was a centre of economy for Verdan, The Diplomatic Centre for political meetings and The Markets for worldwide trade.

The Island would continue to bloom as a place for all.

At the centre lay the last standing portal to Hell. It was decreed that this would be the only one to remain as the others were becoming unstable and bringing elements from other worlds to Verdan. Across the land were still scattered remnants of portals and ruins...
