About

A dimension is one singular connected surface, like the overworld or nether.

Overworld

The main dimension everyone thinks of, inhabited by humans and animals.

Nether

The dimension with a bit more heat.

Deep Nether

The void above the nether roof.

End

The dimension inhabited by creatures under the influence of the conqueror.

Void

The big emptiness underneath the End.

Deep Void

The dimension that exists in pockets below the void. This is where ender chests store their contents.
