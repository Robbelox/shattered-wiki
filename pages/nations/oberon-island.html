# Oberon Island ## About Oberon Island is an island in the [[Three Continents]] of [[Verdan]], raised from the earth by [[Oberon]] himself as neutral ground for all nations. Whatever the political climate of the nations, all are welcome here. As the world around him grew, so did the island. The [[oberon-emerald-bank|Emerald Bank]] became a centre of economy for Verdan, the [[oberon-diplomatic-centre|Diplomatic Centre]] a space for political meetings, and the [[oberon-markets|Markets]] a hub for worldwide trade. Under Oberon's watch, the island has continued to flourish as a place of peace. At its centre lies the last standing [[oberon-portal|portal]] to the nether. When the others were found to be growing unstable—drawing dangerous elements from other worlds into Verdan—it was decreed that this one alone would remain.
