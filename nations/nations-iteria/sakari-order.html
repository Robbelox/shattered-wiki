Military

Members who serve in the military get extra pay, as it is considered a job. The ranks of the military are General, Soldiers, Engineers. Generals organize the whole forces and are paid to keep soldiers and engineers focused and trained. Some generals are tacticians. Soldiers fight for the army, while Engineers construct forts and earthworks to optimize battle conditions.

Economy

The Sakari Order uses Union Leones; which is also used by Eterna. There are 128 Union Leones minted every 2 weeks.

Political Standings

The Sakari Order seeks peace, justice, and prosperity. It has agreements with all factions of the west to protect peace.
