This group had bases and outposts all around Iteria for several years, but unfortunately, many fell to monsters and people alike. Eventually, the Gatesmen group died out, with only the Harbinger left. He tried to recruit more, but he could never manage to rebuild the Gatesmen.

That is, until Ashari Oakenheart. Ashari was out exploring, searching to the north of Avisburg in the great swamps that lay there. Here is where he came across an old Gatesmen base. The base had fallen to ruin, but he was able to find a basement under the debris. Ashari trekked through the mucky water and found several items of value: two books, one belonging to a cultist that had perished in the basement, and the other written by the Harbinger himself.

There was one more point of interest in that basement, however. An altar made of purpur and endstone, with an artifact of extraordinary rarity and potential power floating above it. When Ashari reached out to touch this artifact, he was given a vision of the Conqueror and decided to attempt to make his own branch of the Gatesmen in order to restart the organization and further their goals. In the meanwhile, he put out the goal to build a big capital in a 1001x1001 dug-out perimeter, which put the Gatesmen to work.

During this endeavor, Ashari tried to fulfill a ritual with the altar but made a mistake which eventually caused his demise. Shortly after Ashari's death, The Harbinger retook charge of The Gatesmen, but not for long. When The Harbinger was cured and rechanged into Ebbe, Hundal decided to take the leadership upon himself as he hadn't seen Ashari in weeks. His mission became making the Gatesmen a safe haven for people in the situation he experienced as a child. He took Ashari's mission to build a capital and divided it into smaller and more enjoyable steps.

Projects

[I] Project Evolution

His first plan of action was cancelling the dig and starting up the first project, Project Evolution. This project exists out of 3 tasks: The Cleanup, The Renovation and The Castle. The main goal of this project was to clean up the mess littered around and improving the already existing infrastructure.

Task 1: The Cleanup
The Pit was covered up.
All the out-of-place blocks inside the perimiter were removed.
All littered chests inside the perimiter were gathered and put into the Basement Storage Room.

Task 2: The Renovation
The Gatesmen Basement was redecorated.
Rooms were built in The Gatesmen Basement.
A smeltery was installed in The Gatesmen Basement.
The Gatesmen Basement Prison and Investigation Room were improved.

Task 3: The Castle
The Gatesmen Gate was installed.
The Gatesmen Elevator was installed.
The Gatesmen Tower got a make-over.
The Gatesmen Tower became part of a castle.
A moat was dug around The Gatesmen Castle.
Trees were planted around the Gatesmen Castle.
