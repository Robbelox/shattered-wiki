Villagers

Originally known as the Mutes, and still known as such in some circles, they were human beings twisted by the Nasyrik Plague, a sickness caused by the first Shattering.

Descendants of the original Mutes, they are a peaceful people who typically look for little more than to trade.

Illagers
Illagers

Originally known as the Mutes, and still known as such in some circles, they were human beings twisted by the Nasyrik Plague, a sickness caused by the first Shattering.

A cousin of the Villagers, the Illagers branched only a few generations after the original Nasyrik spread across Ayka. They are war-mongering and violent, originally upset by society shunning them for their condition. That mistreatment bred a culture of violence and competition, which fuels their modern day pillaging.

Piglins
Piglins

About

A pig-like humanoid species native to the Nether. Due to having evolved in the Nether's life-sustaining environment, they are incapable of surviving outside of it without zombifying. Due it's life-giving properties, Piglins see the material "gold" in an almost holy view.

History

They were on peaceful terms with inhabitants of the Overworld for many years, even having permanent trade agreements with the ancient Bastaernians. However, when war from extra-dimensional invaders began to ravish the Nether, the portals connecting the two were severed, primarily due to the influence of Achaea and the Tiberians. Ever since being abandoned in their time of need, Piglins have held a deep distrust and hatred of humanity. The written language of the Piglins was scarcely used even in ancient times, and is unheard of in present day, as such all most to all history is oral.

Culture

Piglin society can be broken into Clans and Houses. A House is a family unit, typically consisting of anywhere from ~10-40 members. A single member of the House is chosen to lead and represent them. A Clan is a collection of typically 3-9 Houses, with a single House Leader chosen to be a Clan Leader, with the other House Leaders acting as advisors. Leaders are chosen not just for strength, but for charisma, wisdom, and cunning. They are a nomadic species, unable to make permanent settlements in the hostile environment, and as such these groups will tend to skew on the lower population side.

Wither Skeletons
Wither Skeletons

Sentries created by the Ender. It is unclear whether they were Ender themselves or not, only that they were at least corrupted by them. In death, the Ender's magic they once had was twisted into a withering touch, toxic to anything they touch.

Wither
Wither

A golem-like entity, created using souls and the corrupted magic of the Wither Skeletons. In it's creation, the souls used to construct it are compressed into a star-like crystal, known as a Nether Star.
